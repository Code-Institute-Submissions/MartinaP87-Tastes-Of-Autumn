This year, the beautiful natural park of Vallombrosa the first edition of Tastes of Autumn Food Festival.

The event will take place on the 15th and 16th of November. The first day will be dedicated to an immersive experience into nature, and the second day will be all about fun and celebration!

The market will be open from 9:30 until 22:00, giving you the chance to try all the traditional dishes of this season and buy some local craftwork.

The initiative aims to raise awareness of our territory, develop a deeper respect for the nature that surrounds us and strengthen the bond with it and with our roots.

This is a splendid occasion to gather people of all ages enhancing the sense of community through cooperative activities, folkloric stories and obviously amazing traditional food...

We also want to welcome tourists from all over the world, offering two days that will give you a closer look and a bigger taste of the core of our land.
