Day 1
Time Activities

10:00

Mushrooms Picking

Perfect for all the family, adventure into the woods with the assistance of an experienced local guide, testing your luck and good eye to spot these little hidden gems!

12:00

Speech From The Major

Our major will talk about our territory and its protection plans. After the speech there will also be the chance to have an open debate, so bring your ideas to improve the environment!

14:00

Arboretum And Giants Trecking

You can visit the most important Italian collection of plants grown for scientific and experimental purposes. There are about 5000 specimens divided into over 700 species of plants and trees. Keep walking and you’ll be able to see The Giants! These are the tallest trees in Italy, reaching 60 meters in height!

16:00

Chestnut Time

For those who can still walk and for the late arrivals the haunt for chestnuts begins! Our guide will walk you to the forest section where you’ll find plenty, take it easy, there’s enough for everyone! This time is not about how many you can spot, it’s about how many you can carry!

18:00

Violin Concert

Time to relax and reward yourself by enjoying some suave music with a good glass of wine

21:00

Folk Music And Dances

Now that we have drunk and eaten enough it’s time to dance it off in the best old fashioned way! Our local band will showcase their repertoire, playing everything from classic traditional music to recent times banger tunes.

Day 2
Time Activities

10:00

Wood Craft

Start your day with a wood workshop where you’ll learn how to use craftsman's tools to shape a piece of wood into a piece of art.

12:00

Jugglers

Colourful and bright, the jugglers they are here to bring you joy and smiles! Two hours of magic, tricks, and acrobatic performances.

14:00

Granma-ster Chef

A deadly competition where 12 ladies over 60, accompanied by a daughter or a son, will fight for the title of best Torta della Nonna ( Grandmother’s cake) of the year. The merciless judges will be 5 highly skilled 10 years olds, very well self-trained in the art of eating cakes, and notorious for their brutal honesty. Don’t worry, after the competition, there will be cake for everyone!

16:00

Puppets Show

A representation of one of the most famous Tuscany tales, a puppet show about a puppet...

18:00

Storyteller

Myths and legends of local folklore will be brought back to life by our narrators, another enchanting moment to experience the magic around us

20:00

Rock Concert

The festival ends with our local Rock band Fool For Fall, the last power kick before bedtime.
